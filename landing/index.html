How it works

1

Connect your tools

Sign in with Google, Slack, Linear, GitHub, or Notion. Cockpit uses OAuth — it never sees your passwords.

2

Everything lands in one feed

Calendar events, Slack messages, PRs, issues, and docs stream into a single timeline. Sorted by relevance, updated live.

3

Ask questions, get real answers

Chat with an AI that searches across all your connected tools before responding. It cites sources so you can verify.

4

Build skills that repeat

Turn recurring tasks into one-click skills — weekly reports, investor updates, standup summaries. Cockpit learns what you need.

Your data stays on your machine

Cockpit runs locally. OAuth tokens are stored in ~/.cockpit with 0600 permissions. No telemetry by default. Client secrets never ship in the app — token exchange goes through a secure proxy.
